Stuck on how to make your project memorable and impactful? Need to figure out how an experience should work or look? Let's brainstorm together.

The successful execution of a creative project often requires substantial coordination. With my background in business and management, I know how to corral the people, conjure the spreadsheets, and do what it takes to get it done.

From intimate workshops to large-scale festivals, I thrive on designing experiences that engage and delight participants in unexpected ways.

Our world is facing innumerable challenges and can be hard to get people's attention on the issues that really matter. Want to engage people publicly and politically? Let's take shenanigans to the streets.
